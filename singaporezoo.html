Singapore Zoo

Singapore Zoo Information

Welcome to Singapore Zoo
Located in the heart of Singapore, the Singapore Zoo is one of the world’s premier wildlife parks. Known for its innovative, open-concept exhibits, it offers an immersive experience where visitors can explore diverse ecosystems and encounter over 2,800 animals from 300 species. The zoo is renowned for its commitment to conservation and educating the public about wildlife protection.

A Glimpse into History
Opened in 1973, the Singapore Zoo has grown from a modest animal park into a world-class wildlife sanctuary. Over the years, it has gained international acclaim for its pioneering approach to animal care and exhibits, offering an open and naturalistic environment for both the animals and visitors. Today, it continues to lead the way in conservation efforts and provides a safe haven for endangered species, while also inspiring future generations to care about wildlife.

What Makes Singapore Zoo Special
The Singapore Zoo is renowned for its open-concept enclosures, which allow animals to roam freely in habitats that closely resemble their natural environments. This unique design enhances the visitors’ experience by offering unobstructed views and fostering a sense of connection with the wildlife. With conservation at its heart, the zoo is involved in several global and local programs aimed at protecting endangered species. It also provides a variety of educational experiences through animal talks, interactive sessions, and immersive exhibits such as Rainforest Walk and Wild Africa.




Singapore Zoo Facilities

Facilities
Facilities at Singapore Zoo:
1. Animal Exhibits:
The zoo houses more than 2,800 animals from over 300 species. Key exhibits include the Rainforest Walk, Fragile Forest, and Wild Africa, where visitors can explore a range of ecosystems and meet wildlife from around the world.
2. Interactive Experiences:
Visitors can enjoy a range of interactive experiences such as animal feeding sessions, keeper talks, and behind-the-scenes tours. These activities offer an in-depth look at the zoo's animals and the conservation programs in place.
3. Dining Options:
The zoo offers a variety of dining options throughout its grounds, from jungle-themed cafes to casual eateries. Guests can enjoy delicious meals while surrounded by lush greenery and the sounds of the jungle.
4. Lodging Options:
While there are no traditional hotels directly within the Singapore Zoo, the Mandai area, which includes the zoo and its related attractions, offers several lodging options for visitors. These options provide a range of experiences from luxury stays to more immersive nature-themed accommodations.

Lodging: If you're planning to spend the day or more exploring the Singapore Zoo and its nearby attractions, there are several lodging options close by to enhance your stay. These accommodations provide a convenient location, allowing you to enjoy both the zoo and the surrounding natural beauty of the Mandai region.




1. Mandai Safari Lodges
Mandai Safari Lodge, opened in 2012, is located near the Singapore Zoo and the Night Safari, providing visitors with a unique stay amidst nature. It was developed to offer a convenient and immersive experience for those visiting the zoo and surrounding wildlife parks. The lodge features eco-friendly accommodations designed to blend with the natural surroundings, further enhancing Singapore's reputation as a hub for sustainable tourism.


Frontier Cabins:
These cozy cabins provide a rustic lodging experience near the main hotel, offering a tranquil and immersive connection with nature.

Map Room:
The hotel's signature Map Room features a large wooden map of the United States, constructed from 15 different kinds of wood from nine countries, offering a unique and visually stunning feature.

Winter View:
The lodge remains open during the cooler months, offering unique views of the surrounding nature and wildlife, making it ideal for winter getaways.



2.The Orchard Hotel Singapore :
The Orchard Hotel Singapore is a luxury hotel located along the famous Orchard Road, known for its prime shopping and dining district. It offers elegant rooms, a variety of dining options, and excellent amenities like a pool and fitness center.

Premier Rooms:
These luxurious rooms offer modern amenities and stylish decor, providing a comfortable and relaxing stay after a day at the zoo.
Dining Experience:
Enjoy a variety of fine dining options, featuring both international and local dishes that cater to a wide range of tastes.
Wellness Facilities:
The hotel includes wellness facilities such as a fitness center and a swimming pool, offering guests the perfect place to unwind and rejuvenate.
