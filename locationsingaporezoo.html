Singapore Zoo

Location
The Singapore Zoo is located at 80 Mandai Lake Road, Singapore 729826. It is situated in the Mandai area, surrounded by lush greenery and natural landscapes.

Timings
The Singapore Zoo operates from 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM every day, including weekends and public holidays. Last entry is typically at 5:30 PM to allow visitors time to explore the exhibits before closing.
