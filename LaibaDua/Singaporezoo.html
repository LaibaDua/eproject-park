Singapore Zoo

Singapore Zoo

Welcome to Singapore Zoo
Located in the heart of Singapore, the Singapore Zoo is one of the world’s premier wildlife parks. Known for its innovative, open-concept exhibits, it offers an immersive experience where visitors can explore diverse ecosystems and encounter over 2,800 animals from 300 species. The zoo is renowned for its commitment to conservation and educating the public about wildlife protection.

A Glimpse into History
Opened in 1973, the Singapore Zoo has grown from a modest animal park into a world-class wildlife sanctuary. Over the years, it has gained international acclaim for its pioneering approach to animal care and exhibits, offering an open and naturalistic environment for both the animals and visitors. Today, it continues to lead the way in conservation efforts and provides a safe haven for endangered species, while also inspiring future generations to care about wildlife.

What Makes Singapore Zoo
Singapore Zoo boasts an array of attractions that captivate millions of visitors each year:

  • The Unique Open-Concept Design of Singapore Zoo: The zoo features open-concept enclosures where animals live in habitats resembling their natural environments, enhancing the visitor experience. .
  • Connecting with Wildlife: Unobstructed views allow visitors to connect with animals in environments that feel natural and unconfined.
  • Endangered Species Programs: The zoo participates in programs dedicated to safeguarding endangered species both locally and globally..




Singapore Zoo Facilities

Facilities
Singapore Zoo offers a variety of facilities to enhance visitor experiences, including :

  • Night Safari:Experience the zoo’s wildlife in a completely different way during the Night Safari, where animals are active after dark..


  • Safari Adventurers:A unique, interactive experience where visitors can join behind-the-scenes tours to learn about animal care and conservation.


  • Camping at Singapore Zoo:Enjoy an overnight stay in the zoo with its "Wild Safari" camping experience, offering a chance to sleep under the stars while surrounded by nature.


  • Animal Encounters:Get up close with various animals through specially organized animal encounter sessions, providing a hands-on learning experience.


  • Rainforest Kidzworld:A family-friendly area featuring water play zones, animal encounters, and educational games for children.


  • Educational Workshops and Programs:The zoo offers various workshops and programs, including guided tours, animal talks, and interactive sessions to enhance learning about wildlife..
