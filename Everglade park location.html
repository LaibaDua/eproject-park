Everglades National Park

Location
Everglades National Park is located in southern Florida, USA. It spans across multiple counties, including Miami-Dade, Monroe, and Collier. The park is situated at the southern tip of the Florida Peninsula and covers a vast area of wetlands, mangroves, and unique ecosystems.
